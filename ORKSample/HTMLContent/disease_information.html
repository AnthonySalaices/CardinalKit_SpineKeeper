Key Points about back pain:

Most back pain will go away within a few days or weeks.

Back pain can occur anywhere in the spine, but is most common in the lower back or lumbar spine.

Most back pain is not due to a serious illness.

Back pain can be due to muscle strains, disc issues, trauma, arthritis, bone disease, aging and other causes.

Many people who get back pain will have another episode of back pain within 2 years

Bedrest for too long is bad for back pain - stay active!
