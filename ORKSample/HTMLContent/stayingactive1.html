• The sooner you get active and mobile the better. If your pain is particularly severe, you may need rest and a short time off work. However, even then you can likely achieve most of your daily activities if you develop a plan. Identify the challenges and ways things can be done differently.
  • The key to the right activity is to increase mobility without putting too much strain on your back. The principles are as follows:
    • Keep moving.
    • Do not remain in one position for too long.
