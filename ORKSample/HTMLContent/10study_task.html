At the beginning of the study, you may choose from or choose to be assigned one of two lifestyle modification tracks to help manage your back pain. The tracks include 28 days of exercises and education to help you learn how to manage your back pain. Each day, you will also be asked to answer questions or perform app based measurements of your function to help track your progress.

Some of the tracks will involve increasing physical activity and performing excercises. Please talk with your doctor before increasing physical activity or assesing fitness.
